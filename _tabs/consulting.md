## What I do

I'm the senior technical fellow you bring in when your team can't move past a hard core problem.

Not a vertical specialist, not a strategist, not someone you hire to convince a stakeholder or run a team. The offer is solving the problem itself — the part where the existing approach is stuck and the path forward isn't obvious.

## Where I'm strongest

- **Real-time AI** — generative models, RL, and small LLMs deployed under tight latency and compute budgets, including in shader-level pipelines.
- **Computer vision** — classical and learned, on imagery from satellite to ground-level.
- **Geospatial intelligence** — large-area analytics, pipeline architecture, and turning spatial problems into tractable non-spatial ones.
- **Graph theory** — both theoretical and applied (patented framework, prior commercial product).
- **High-performance simulation** — heterogeneous GPU clusters, physics-informed models, real-time scale.

## A few problems I've solved

- **Parallel radiative transfer on heterogeneous GPU clusters**, integrated with NASA's GEOS-5 atmospheric model. Co-PI on the work.
- **Infrared scene simulator** for situational awareness — physics-faithful synthetic IR imagery at simulation speed.
- **Building-footprint extraction** at continental scale from aerial and satellite imagery.
- **A holistic, lossless geospatial pipeline** that partitions an area of interest into contiguous blocks of uniform information, making spatial operations queryable as non-spatial aggregations from no-SQL stores. The technical core of a company I founded and sold.
- **Wildfire modeling and landcover-change detection** at scale.

## How engagements tend to look

Short, focused, technically deep. Often a few weeks of investigation and prototyping that produces a working approach your team can run with — not a deck.
