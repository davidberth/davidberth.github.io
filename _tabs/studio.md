## In development

Lixel Studio is the home for owned software and creative work: tools, apps, and games developed independently rather than for clients.

Studio sits downstream of [Research](/research/). Each project here tends to start as a research thread that has matured into a tool or product. *The Dimming*, for example, grows out of the latent procedural graphics work being developed on the research side; future Studio projects are likely to emerge from the graph-coloring program in similar fashion.

The first project under this banner is *The Dimming*, a planet-scale game built around real-time generative AI and a partial-order system of intelligence and agency. More on it, and on Studio as a whole, when it is ready to show.

For now, occasional progress notes appear under [Writing](/writing/).
